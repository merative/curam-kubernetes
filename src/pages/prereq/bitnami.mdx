Bitnami provides one-click installers and pre-packaged application stacks for popular open-source applications. Recently, Bitnami has changed its licensing model and now requires a **paid license for production use**. More information can be found on their [GitHub issue](https://github.com/bitnami/charts/issues/35164) and their [website](https://bitnami.com/).

In **Curam**, Bitnami components are used in several places, most notably for the creation and management of keys and secrets such as **wlpadm**, **LTPA keys**, and the use of `bitnamisecure/apache-exporter` in the **web** and **uawebapp** charts.

Customers are responsible for reviewing their use of Bitnami components and ensuring compliance with Bitnami’s licensing.
